When to Call the Vet Immediately

Some situations require urgent veterinary attention. Do not wait if your goat shows any of the following:

Down and unable to stand
Severe bloat (tight left side)
Straining to urinate (UC emergency)
Labored kidding (over 30 min pushing)
Temperature over 105°F or under 100°F
Seizures or severe staggering
Suspected poisoning
Heavy bleeding or deep wounds
